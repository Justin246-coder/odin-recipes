Chicken Alfredo

Description

In today's recipe, I am going to show you how to make delicious Chicken Alfredo. Chicken Alfredo is by far one of the most popular Italian dishes on the planet, loved by many people worldwide. The delicious meal was invented by a man named Alfredo Di Lelio. Di Lelio came up with the famous dish in Rome in 1908. How did Chicken Alfredo become so popular?. Back in the 1920s, Hollywood stars such as Douglas Fairbanks and Mary Pickford fell in love with the dish, they spread the word around Hollywood then it took the United States by storm.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into chunks
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 (16 ounce) packages thin spaghetti
  • 4 (15 ounce) jars Alfredo sauce
  • 2 tablespoons Italian seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons chopped onion
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • 2 tablespoons salt
  • 2 tablespoons ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • ½ tablespoon cayenne pepper, or to taste
  • 1 ½ cups shredded Parmesan cheese (Optional)
  • 2 cups chopped broccoli (Optional
