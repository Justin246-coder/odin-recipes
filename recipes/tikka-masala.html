Tikka Masala

Description

Get ready to learn how to make delicious Tikka Masala. With it's grilled chunks of chicken enveloped in a spiced, velvety orange curry, Chicken Tikka Masala is one of the most popular dishes in Britain. It's a bestselling ready meal at supermarket Sainsbury's, which sells 1.6 million Chicken Tikka Masala meals every year and stocks 16 related products. I look forward to you making this delicious meal for family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can chopped tomatoes
  • 4 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • 2 cloves garlic roughly chopped
  • 1 (1 inch) piece ginger, coarsley chopped
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons masala curry paste
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Â¼ cup water
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

Steps

  1. Combine tomatoes, yogurt, garlic, and ginger in a blender and process until smooth

  2. Heat oil in a large frying pan over medium heat. Add onion and fry until soft. 3 to 4 minutes, stirring constantly. Stir in curry paste and fry until fragant, 1 minute more, stirring once or twice. Add the tomato mixture and chicken to the pan and mix together. Season with salt and pepper. Remove pan from heat.

  3. Mix water anf flour together in a bowl. Stir into and the chicken mixture in frying pan. Return pan to heat and bring to a boil, stirring constantly, about 5 minutes. Reduce heat to low, cover, and cook until thickened, about 15 minutes more. Sprinkle with cilantro and serve immediately.
